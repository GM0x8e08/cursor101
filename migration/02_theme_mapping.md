# Theme Mapping — 4 Themes + 19-Article Assignment

## The 4 themes

### 1. Physical Supply & Infrastructure
The physical reality of compute: chips, memory, power, datacenters, supply shortages, buildout, Bitcoin-miner-to-AI-DC conversion.
Ports/refines old: Energy & Power Bottleneck, Demand-Side Procurement.

### 2. Compute Quality & Measurement
Is compute uniform and how do we measure it: silicon lottery, hardware variance, performance benchmarking frameworks.
Ports/refines old: Silicon Lottery & Hardware Variance + Compute Quality & Verification (merged).

### 3. Pricing, Markets & Derivatives
Treating compute as a priceable/tradable asset: price indices, forward curves, futures/derivatives, exchanges, market structure & sizing, the asset-class / semi-fungibility framework.
Ports/refines old: Forward Curve & Pricing + Compute Derivatives & Exchanges.

### 4. Capital & Risk
Financing the buildout: GPU-backed lending, residual value, Nvidia backstop, valuation frameworks.
Ports/refines old: GPU-Backed Lending & Risk.

**B vs C dividing line:** B = performance measurement; C = price. "Building a Robust GPU Index" is a price index → C. "SiliconMark vs InferenceX" / "GPU Cloud Lottery" are performance benchmarks → B.
